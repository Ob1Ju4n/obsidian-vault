Recap

Alex wakes up slowly. She soon finds that she’s detained in a dark and damp room. Slowly, she begins to remember details of the night before that allow her to understand better what happened and realizes that she might be in a very difficult situation.

To keep in Mind:

- This is a survival horror setting highly influenced by the resident evil games franchise
    
- The system used is Ironsworn/Delve + Starforged
    
- Iron Vow: Alex needs irrefutable evidence of the shady business of a big pharma corporation that involves unethical research on humans. She aims to bring them to justice, preventing any potential harm to citizens.
