## The System

We’ll be using Ironsworn/Delve + Starforged. Most of the moves will come from the Lodestar book but when needed we’ll use the Starforged oracles for inspiration.

## The Setting

This will be a survival horror one-shot, heavily inspired by the Resident Evil games franchise, so, reader beware as I will try to detail horror scenes that might include gore or body horror, oppressive descriptions and cheesy dialogue (or maybe not). ## The Main Character

The very first thing that I do when creating a character is to define its motivation. This time around, I decided to roll for some guidance:

> _Action (95) = Learn_
> 
> _Theme (89) = Truth_

That’s pretty straightforward.

I envision a reporter who has been researching some deals and agreements that will endanger the lives of hundreds of innocents, her included. She’s now trying to **gather evidence to finally expose the perpetrators behind these shady businesses**. We can work with this as Alex’s current vow.

In terms of mechanics, these are the stats:  

> _Name:_ Alex
> 
> Edge **1** | Heart **2** | Iron **1** | Shadow **2** | Wits **3**
> 
> _Assets:_
> 
> - Pretender: This is not her first time as an undercover journalist. In fact, impersonation is one of the tools of the trade.
>     
> - Lorekeeper: No matter the story, she always digs to the bottom of the issues. Clear and complete knowledge is her specialty
>     
> - Scry: She has learned to read the room and connect the dots to draw conclusions.
>     
> 
> All the other status tracks start at their default values.

## Inciting Incident & Starting Scene

_“Alex, an undercover Journalist has been researching shady businesses related to a big pharma corporation. Unfortunately, her efforts have brought the most unwanted retaliation. After being abducted, she wakes up slowly to the sound of alarms, an annoying headache and the reflections of flickering lights that filter into the small room where she’s detained._

_Fully awakened, Alex tries to recall a clue that helps her explain her current situation.”_

I envision that she was with a VIP trying to deceive him to obtain confidential information. This sound like a compel move:

> _Asset: Pretender (10, 2 vs 1 + 2) = Weak Hit_
> 
> _Compel ( 8, 5 vs **1** + 2 Pretender + 1) = Weak Hit_

The compel action die tells us that someone doubts us. I envision that this is how things went sideways:

_“Some memories start to come back. She was drinking with someone from the government. He worked for the military but was not a soldier. They share a couple of drinks and everything was flowing just fine but when she mentioned a topic about the corporation (she can’t recall what she said), she noticed how the man took a more defensive stance. She tried to regain the lost terrain, flirting, while taking the conversation to a totally unrelated subject. At some point she was convinced that he was lowering his guard, maybe because of a smile, or, was it the bourbon? Regardless, they left the bar and went into his car. He opened the door for her and as a true gentleman, closed it making sure she was comfortable. The last thing she remembers is that he opened the rear door behind her, according to him “just to leave the coat” in the backseat._

_After that, fear._

_The faint odor of chloroform tickles her nostrils and and she remembers the pressure and the darkness when everything went black._

_She clearly underestimated him and now is here, in a damp room“_
