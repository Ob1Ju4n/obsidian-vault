### Situation
- The adventurers split to make the most of their time in town.
- [[Alderan]] is headed to the Phandalin Miner's Exchange. He wants to find out why the miners don't seem to be affected by [[The Redbrands Gang]] 
- [[Balian]] is following the leads given by [[Toblen Stonehill|Toblen's]] son as he thinks that there might be clues that help them figure out details on the gang.
- [[Elmina Barthen]] has not returned to the shop and the adventurers are worried about her.

While crossing the town, the sounds of birds chirping mix with the laughs of few kids that run around the town's plaza under the warm sunlight of the mid-day. For a brief moment, [[Alderan]] feels that the town is peaceful and even too quiet. It is perhaps a consequence of not seeing any goblins or bandits around. There's just people living their lives, walking around and running errands. However, a detailed look reveals the mirage. The townspeople have faces with eyes always looking to the ground. Mothers rushing their children inside the houses and whispers instead of greetings. It's clear that the people in [[Phandalin]] don't feel safe and they do not know what to do next. [[Alderan]] ask to himself if there's no leader that can stand-up for his people. That's when the building with the bell on top catches his attention. If there's someone that can explain better why [[The Redbrands Gang]] took control of town, he must be in there.

Instead of going directly to the Miner's Exchange, [[Alderan]] walks towards the Townmaster's Hall.
