### Situation
- _Welcome to [[Phandalin]] p.27_
- The adventurers travel at a fast pace and after 5 hours, with some sunlight still, they arrive to town from the Triboar trail to the north with a wagon full of wares, a couple of horses and oxen beasts.
- [[The Redbrands Gang]], a squad of ruffians is running the town. They tend to be the most active during the night although some of them patrol around town during the day.
- The night is near. Kids playing on the street are called by their parents, others disappear behind some houses as soon as the wagon enters the town while some folks hurry to complete unfinished errands before the shops close.

### Development

_Does any member of [[The Redbrands Gang]] comes out to meet the adventurers?_

> _Ask the Oracle / Likely (97) = **Yes**_
> _Character Descriptors (~~42~~, 49, 19) = **~~Timid~~, Charming, Suspicious**_

[[Balian]]  is conscious but doesn't talk much. He's been feeling more pain but at the same time is relieved that at last they got to [[Phandalin]]. [[Alderan]] slows down the cart as a red hooded figure approaches. With an exaggerated bow, a human woman welcomes the party while scanning the wagon. She asks about their business in town pretending to sound like a noble or educated person but the bard knows better and realizes that she's hiding her true self. Still, he mentions that he's looking for Barthen's Provisions and a place to rest, careful to avoid giving away any other details. The woman walks them to the shop and asks the clerk for the owner. 

Soon after, a young woman appears and introduces herself as [[Elmina Barthen]]. After she greets the adventurers she turns to the hooded lady an looks at her with a disgusted face, asking if she will stay to check the cargo as usual. With a fake salute, the pretender agrees and follows the clerk to the back of the transport. 

Some minutes later a heated discussion is settled with some coin and the red hooded woman leaves, thanking the adventures for their business in town and wishing them good luck on his way back. [[Elmina Barthen]] then invites the party inside the shop to pay her debt and share a drink while they talk for a bit. 

> [[Alderan]] Reach a Milestone
> Mark Progress
> Alderan's Delivery Service - Troublesome: ~~1-2-3-4-5-6-7-8-9~~-0

When coming inside she notices that [[Balian]] is badly wounded and immediately sends one of the clerks to look for [[Sister Garaele]]. Meanwhile, [[Alderan]] re narrates the battle and mentions that [[Gundren Rockseeker]] and his companion could've been abducted by the Goblins. [[Balian]] shows the tainted blade and gives some more details regarding the slain creature with green eyes.  

> [[Alderan]] Fulfill Your Vow (8,9 vs 9) = Weak Hit
> +1 to next Iron Vow

[[Elmina Barthen]] seems anxious and upset after the conversation. She tells the adventurers that she expected [[Gundren Rockseeker]], her friend, much earlier, as they planned to finish a conversation regarding [[The Lost Mine of Phandelver]]. Now she is really worried for his wellbeing. She also mentions that [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren's]] brothers have not returned to town, and so, they won't be able to help locate his sibling. On top of that, everyone in town has to deal with the abuse of [[The Redbrands Gang]] but no one feels safe, specially after witnessing how Thel, one of the artisans in town, was murdered  when he stood up for his wife and soon who later became missing.

The silence sets in the room. The adventurers have already lived through similar situations in their younger years, specially [[Alderan]] who has jumped from town to town, but despite the grim outlook, they both know what needs to be done. [[Balian]] stands up in pain and with a solemn voice, he ensures the woman that, as his duty demands, he will drive the ruffians out of town no matter the cost. [[Alderan]] hesitates but can't help but feel inspired by the ranger's words, so he ends up joining him.

A glimpse of hope illuminates [[Elmina Barthen|Elmina's]] face. At first she showed resignation but that changed quickly when she saw the clerk came back with the [[Sister Garaele|Priestess]] who ,after a brief introduction, started to prepare herself to tend [[Balian]]'s wounds. 

[[Alderan]] joined her:

> _+1 Momentum (Loyalist)_
> _[[Balian]] makes another move with +1_

> _[[Balian]] Sojourn/Rest (3, 10 vs 3 + 1 Aid + 2 Heart) = **Weak Hit**_

The priestess and the bard work together and manage to provide some relief to the elven ranger, however, there's no doubt that the wound was magical and whatever incantation was used on the weapon, it's something that the cleric couldn't recognize. She warns them that there are possible unknown side effects and promises them that she will try to gather more information. 

As the busy day comes to an end, [[Sister Garaele]] prepares to head back to the temple. Aware of the elvish manners, [[Balian]] offers his services instead of gold as payment. She thinks about it for a second and accepts the aid, asking him and his friend to stop by the plaza in about a ten-day, as she also has urgent matters to attend out of town. [[Elmina Barthen]] nods and thanks her for her assistance. After that, she prepares some space behind the shop for the adventurers to rest as in her own words: 

*"it is best to avoid unwanted attention from [[The Redbrands Gang]]. They don't like adventurers and while their numbers might dwindle during the day, their forces and their rudeness multiply once the alcohol starts flowing and the shadows conceal their misdeeds". 

Once the woman leaves, the duo discuss their approach. They are outnumbered and they don't know the place. [[Alderan]] is optimistic and very confident on the skills of the ranger and believes that the townsfolk just need a push that allows them to see that the gang is only strong because they let them be. [[Balian]] who has outlived the bard, has a different experience, therefore, he suggest that there must be a leader that they have to find and that must be dealt with.

The next day, the duo wakes up in the early morning as [[Elmina Barthen]] arrives to the shop. Tired, sad and frustrated, she asks again, almost begging them to return and find the dwarf. They notice her concern but for them [[The Redbrands Gang]] is a far bigger issue. She can't disagree but doesn't give up and makes a proposal mentioning that being a local, she can gather more information about the gang while they search for [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]. The duo does not want to risk an innocent life, however, before they can refuse, she tells them that this is also her town and she has to help in one way or another. 

[[Balian]] sighs. He has seen that stubborn expression many times, usually on dwarves but in recent centuries adopted more and more by humans. In contrast, [[Alderan]] admires the courage of the shopkeeper and gives her some words of reassurance.

> [[Alderan]] Swear an Iron Vow (8, 10 + 1 + 1 Fulfill + 4 Heart) = Miss
> In Search of [[Gundren Rockseeker]] - Formidable: 1-2-3-4-5-6-7-8-9-0
> -2 Momentum

They end up accepting and ask the woman to be careful and stay out of danger. 

Shortly after, with the horses ready and just before they head out, [[Elmina Barthen]] gives them some rations and supplies, wishing them good luck and hoping for the best.

[[Balian]] presses his heels against the horse and gallops back towards the Triboar trail to the north, followed closely by the loyal bard and the picking eyes of the few people that roam early in town.
