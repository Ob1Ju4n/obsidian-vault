### Hooks
After a beautiful performance in one of the many taverns that exist in the city of Neverwinter, [[Alderan]] made a deal with a dwarf named [[Gundren Rockseeker]] to bring a wagonload of cargo to a town to the south named [[Phandalin]]. Initially, the dwarf was reluctant as the Bard was alone, however, after some drinks, food, laughs and some clever words [[Alderan]] was able to convince him. He asked for 30gp as he had to arrange an additional escort but in the end the deal was settled at 25gp.

[[Gundren Rockseeker]] and his own escort, a warrior named [[Sildar Hallwinter]], set out ahead of [[Alderan]]. The dwarf claimed that he had "business to take care of" and the bard was still asleep. 

Later that day, still recovering from the hangover, [[Alderan]] wandered around town looking for a suitable adventurer who didn't charge much. He approached warriors, mages and even a cleric but their prices were too high. Still, with the luck on his side, he finally secured the services of a ranger named [[Balian]], an elf member of a "guild" specialized in protecting travelers across the realm as well as the small settlements and towns along [[The SwordCoast]]. He was just preparing to travel himself back to the south.

Once the bard and the ranger made the deal (12gp for [[Balian]]) they set off to [[Phandalin]]
