### Situation
- The adventurers entered [[The Cragmaw Cave]] using an opening above ground
- They're lying on top of a ledge. It's difficult to see below as smoke covers the place, however, there's a lot of noise coming from what sounds like a group of Goblins.
- There's one annoying voice, loudest among the group of creatures that lies below but neither the bard or the ranger can understand  what they say.
- What they clearly hear is the suffering voice of [[Sildar Hallwinter]], the dwarf's human escort. He begs for water but the Goblins doesn't seem to care.

On their way back, [[Alderan]] starts to talk with [[Sildar Hallwinter|Sildar]] and as soon as they focus on the latest events, he goes from a momentary relief to a pensive and frustrated mood. He reveals that he is an agent of [[The Lords Alliance]] seeking ways to make settlements thrive, so when he was approached by the Rockseeker brothers, he thought that helping them would  benefit [[Phandalin]] for sure. Then, clearly disappointed, he blames himself for being unable to protect his colleague. After a lengthy silence, it is the bard who tries to redirect to conversation, asking if helping the dwarves was somehow related to [[The Lost Mine of Phandelver]].

[[Sildar Hallwinter]] seemed surprised by the question as he had not mentioned any details regarding his business with the dwarf, but [[Balian]] who noticed [[Sildar Hallwinter|Sildar's]] reaction, told him about what [[Elmina Barthen]] mentioned back in town. [[Alderan]] joined him adding some detail about [[The Redbrands Gang]]
