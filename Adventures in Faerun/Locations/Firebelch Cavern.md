### Site Details

#### Rank
Dangerous

#### Theme

#### Domain

#### General Features
- Cave is dark unless stated otherwise
- The tunnels have a height between 4 and 11ft and are uneven making it difficult terrain often with some stalagmites and lavacicles in the way. Pipes & valves can be found across the ceilings of the cave as well as small humanoid footprints
- Vegetation roots can be seen through the rock in sections closer to the surface
- The finished areas have a height of 6ft unless stated otherwise with melted walls
- Gray and black volcanic rock walls sometimes ragged and razorsharp, sometimes smooth  and fissured
