### Goal
Find the whereabouts of [[Gundren Rockseeker]]

### Site Details

#### Rank
Troublesome

#### Theme
Fortified

#### Domain
Cavern

#### General Features
- Steeply sloping ceilings with stalactite-covered chambers rising 20 to 30ft from the floor
- Cave is dark unless stated otherwise
- Stalagmites are common in big chambers
- There's a little stream of running water that runs through the cave and can muffle other sounds
- Some areas have rubble

#### Unique Features
- Klarg's Cave: [[Klarg|Klarg's]] dwelling place where most of the stolen goods are being kept. Usually, it contains 30 days worth of "work"
	- Complication: It's very likely that [[Klarg]] is around.
	- Opportunity: A treasure chest 
- ~~Goblin Den: 
	- ~~Complication: [[Sildar Hallwinter]] is being tormented by the Goblins and seems to be in a pretty rough shape. Goblin bosses use to be very active in this area.
	- ~~Opportunity: One of the goblin bosses may want to free [[Sildar Hallwinter]] if the adventurers take down [[Klarg]]
- Secret Passage: 30ft of a natural formation that looks like a chimney
	- Complication: Steep climb through a narrow chimney. Falling will be harmful
	- Opportunity: The passage communicates directly to Klarg's Cave
