## The Cragmaw Goblins - The Graytusk Orcs

A local Goblin band (squad) that dwells on [[The Cragmaw Cave]] and patrols the Goblin trail and part of the Triboar trail.

They are criminals lead by a bugbear named [[Klarg]]. 

They spend most of their time attacking and stealing from unsuspecting travelers. 

Most of their wealth comes from trading the wares they steal, usually for food, weapons and on very rare occasions for uncommon/magical items.

From time to time they get special "orders" from the monarch of [[The Crawmag Clan]], [[King Grol]] himself.

### Projects / Goals
Grow until becoming a platoon capable of raiding towns and settlements.
