## The Redbrands Gang - The Cardinals Gang

A criminal gang (platoon) that goes extorting and bullying from town to town. Their hideout is located in a dungeon complex under [[Tresendar Manor]]

They are ruffians that use a distinctive red hood and are led by a human wizard known as [[Glasstaff]] 

They spend most of their days sleeping the hangover of the night before, however, some of them are ordered to stay vigilant during the day to keep the town under control. They resort to extortion, bullying or other more serious threats in order to achieve this goal.

They consider themselves the "owners" of the town. As long as everyone is under control they don't have any major needs or ambitions apart from gathering more wealth from the townsfolk.

### Projects / Goals
Maintain the status quo in town or in other words, drive away or eliminate adventurers or law enforcement of any kind.
