### Appearance (_Features_)
- A Bugbear always accompanied by his pet wolf and two guards who are usually close to him.
- Wears a crown made of bones

### Roleplaying (_Mannerisms_)
- Filled with delusions of grandeur, has been emboldened by his success as raider
- Views himself as a king in the making
- Refers to himself in third person

### Background (_Agenda/Tactics_)
- Dreams of becoming king of [[The Crawmag Clan]] one day
- Wants to amass wealth to fulfill the goal of [[The Cragmaw Goblins]]
- Usually he's guarded by two goblins (his honor guard)
- He's reckless but also is the most feared warrior among [[The Cragmaw Goblins]]. 
- If he notices their minions are losing, he will intervene take the spot
- If his guards and wolf dies, he will try to escape

### Key Info - Optional
- He knows what happened to [[Gundren Rockseeker]] and [[Sildar Hallwinter]](p.18)
- He knows that some strange goblins have joined [[The Cragmaw Goblins]] raids, however, he doesn't know where they come from. As capable as they seem to be, he feels they're a threat.
