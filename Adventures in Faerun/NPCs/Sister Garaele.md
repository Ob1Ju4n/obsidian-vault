### Appearance (_Features_)
- Tall Elven woman (based upon _Stephanie Cayo_).
- Wears a long silver kaftan that enhance her height, a flower tiara and a short golden necklace with a disc.
- She's a cleric of Tymora and an agent of [[The Harpers]] 

### Roleplaying (_Mannerisms_)
- Confident, kind and bold.
- Resilient and wise.
- Talks in idioms.

### Background (_Agenda/Tactics_)
- She has an active duty of recruiting new members for [[The Harpers]]
- She wants to obtain a response from a Banshee named Agatha.

### Key Info - Optional
- She knows that the Banshee is dangerous if anyone approaches her with hostile intentions, however, if proper obeisance is shown, she might be willing to trade information in exchange of the item that she yearns for. A jeweled silver comb.
