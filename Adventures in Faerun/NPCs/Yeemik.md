### Appearance (_Features_)
- A fat goblin with yellow skin, 4ft tall
- Wears a helmet with one horn coming out from one of it's sides

### Roleplaying (_Mannerisms_)
- Treacherous and overconfident. A coward if facing a dire situation
- Smart enough to beg for mercy
- Always shows his teeth while he talks

### Background (_Agenda/Tactics_)
- Second in command in [[The Cragmaw Cave]]
- Avoids getting involved in battle / always sends others to fight for him
- Dreams of becoming the leader of [[The Cragmaw Cave]]
- Wants to topple [[Klarg]] as soon as he has the opportunity

### Key Info - Optional
- He knows where [[Klarg]] dwells
