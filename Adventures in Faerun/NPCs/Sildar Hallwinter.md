### Appearance (_Features_)
- Human, 55 years old.
- Short hair, in good shape, almost 6ft tall
  
### Roleplaying (_Mannerisms_)
- Kind & proactive
- Believes in fairness and the law
- Has military training. 
- Very good at parrying with a sword
- Knows and respects military structure


### Background (_Agenda/Tactics_)
- Former honorable member of [[The Griffon Cavalry of Waterdeep]] and active agent of [[The Lords Alliance]]
- Wants to make it to [[Phandalin]] but because of his wounds, will need some aid to get there
- Wishes to clean the surrounding caves and trails from "vermin"
- _As an agent of the [[The Lords Alliance]]:_
	- Sent to investigate the whereabouts of [[Glasstaff|Iarno Albrek]], who disappeared after arriving in [[Phandalin]]
	- Seeks to assist [[Gundren Rockseeker]] in reopening [[The Lost Mine of Phandelver]] in hopes that this brings wealth and progress to [[Phandalin]]
	- Constantly looks for worthy members that can uphold the values of the faction

### Key Info - Optional
- Believes that all Goblins and Orcs are just bandits. He has no confidence in them.
	- Remembers a specific Goblin that was among the patrol that ambushed them. This one had an elongated skull and a guttural voice. The creature said to him: _"you're not what [[Ruxithid]] wants"_ left the group and has not come back.
- Knows that the dwarves located  [[The Wave Echo Cave]], an entrance to the [[The Lost Mine of Phandelver]]. He also knows that [[Gundren Rockseeker]] carried a map with the secret location
- Heard from the Goblins that [[The Spider]] ordered the ambush and wanted [[Gundren Rockseeker]] to be brought to him. He doesn't know who or what [[The Spider]] is, however
	- He suspects from the Goblin conversations that [[Klarg]] sent [[Gundren Rockseeker]] to the [[The Cragmaw Castle]]. It's possible that [[The Spider]] is there.
